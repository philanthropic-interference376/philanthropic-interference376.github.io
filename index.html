About

Welcome

I am a Full Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Augusta University. My research lies at the intersection of applied mathematics, mathematical biology, epidemiology, ecology, and optimal control theory.

My work develops and analyzes mathematical models for complex biological and disease processes, including infectious disease dynamics, vector-borne diseases, within-host virus dynamics, multi-scale models, ecological systems, and intervention strategies for disease control.
