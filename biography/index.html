Walepenja Joseph is an Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Augusta University. His research focuses on mathematical modeling, analysis, and simulation of biological, ecological, and epidemiological systems.

His work includes applications to infectious diseases, vector-borne diseases, within-host virus dynamics, multi-scale models, delay differential equations, age-structured models, and optimal control.
